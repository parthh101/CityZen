Emergency Tips

  • Stay calm and speak clearly when calling emergency services
  • Provide your exact location and the nature of the emergency
  • Follow the instructions given by the emergency operator
  • Do not hang up until the operator tells you to do so
  • If possible, stay with the victim until help arrives
